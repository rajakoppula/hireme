## Who I am

I build systems that get found. For over thirteen years I've been the person who takes a half-formed idea and turns it into something live, ranking, and earning — whether that's a search engine, an affiliate brand, a SaaS tool, or a content engine that AI models quote as a source.

I learned growth the hard way: by shipping. A wallpaper blog hit **100K monthly visits** in 2013 and I was hooked. Dozens of projects later — some sold, some scaled, some gloriously dead — I've developed an instinct for the one thing that actually matters online: *how things get discovered, and how that discovery compounds into revenue.*

The discovery game just changed completely. Search isn't ten blue links anymore — it's AI answers, cited sources, and answer engines. **Getting found by machines** (GEO, AEO, LLM visibility) is the new SEO, and it's the most exciting problem I've worked on in years.

And these days I don't just hand over a strategy deck. With modern AI tooling I **build and ship the thing itself** — WordPress sites, custom web apps, deployable products — start to finish.
