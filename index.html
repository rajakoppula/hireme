The short version

For 15 years I've been shipping things on the internet. Some became six-figure machines. A few crashed beautifully. Every one taught me how digital products are found, grown, and monetized.

It started back in 2011, and by 2013 I'd pushed a wallpaper blog past 100K visits a month with nothing but curiosity and SEO. That hooked me. Since then I've built torrent metasearch engines, local discovery platforms, affiliate brands, viral content sites, and lead-gen funnels — learning the full stack of growth by doing, breaking, and rebuilding.

Today the game has shifted. Classic SEO is saturated; the real frontier is being visible to machines — getting brands cited by LLMs, surfaced in AI answers, and ranked in answer engines. That's GEO & AEO, and it's the biggest opening in a decade.

And I no longer stop at strategy. With modern AI tooling I design and ship real products — WordPress builds, custom web apps, deployable tools — from idea to live URL. Strategist and builder, same person.
